How to support me via PayPal:
  1. Click "Support Me".
  2. Choose a preset amount ($5, $10, $20) or enter your own.
  3. In the PayPal window:
    • Log in if you have a PayPal account.
    • Or click "Debit or Credit Card" to pay without an account.
  4. Confirm your payment — that’s it!
Secure & simple: PayPal handles payments safely, even without an account.
